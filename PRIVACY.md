# Privacy Policy

**App:** Easy Calorie Tracker
**Developer:** Halswell Apps
**Last updated:** 18 May 2026

This page explains what data Easy Calorie Tracker ("the app") collects,
how it is used, and your choices. The short version: the app does not
collect, transmit, or share any of your data.

## Data collection

The app does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data
on any external server. All food, meal, macro, and weight data you
enter is stored **only on your device**, in the app's local database.

The app does not require an account. You do not need to provide an
email address, name, or any other identifying information to use the
app.

## Permissions

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts,
location, photos, files, or any other sensitive Android permission.

## Third-party services

The app does not use any third-party services for analytics,
advertising, crash reporting, or any other purpose. No third-party
SDKs are bundled with the app.

## Cookies and tracking

The app does not use cookies, tracking pixels, advertising
identifiers, or any other tracking technology.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone,
including children under the age of 13.

## Your data, your control

Because all data is stored locally on your device:
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data.
- Resetting your device permanently deletes all your data.
- Backing up your phone (e.g. Google Backup) may include the app's
  data unless you've turned that off in your phone settings.

There is nothing for the developer to delete on your behalf, because
nothing is ever sent to the developer.

## Changes to this policy

If the app changes in a way that affects your privacy (for example,
if a future version adds an optional feature that connects to a
server), this policy will be updated **before** that version is
released, and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised.
You can always check the current version of this policy at the
repository linked from the app's Google Play listing.
